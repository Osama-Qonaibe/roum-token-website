Regulatory Frameworks

1. GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) - EU

Complete data protection compliance with full user rights including access, modification, and deletion. User consent management, data retention policies, and privacy-by-design principles implemented throughout the platform.

2. AML/KYC (Anti-Money Laundering / Know Your Customer)

Comprehensive customer verification, transaction monitoring, and suspicious activity reporting. Multi-level KYC verification with enhanced due diligence for high-risk users and entities.

3. OFAC (Office of Foreign Assets Control) - US

Sanctions screening against OFAC SDN list, consolidated non-SDN list, and other restricted party lists. Real-time monitoring of transactions and automatic blocking of restricted jurisdictions and entities.

4. FinCEN (Financial Crimes Enforcement Network)

Compliance with US Treasury regulations, Currency Transaction Reports (CTR), and Suspicious Activity Reports (SAR). Regular filing and monitoring protocols for financial crime prevention.

5. SOX (Sarbanes-Oxley) - Corporate Governance

Financial reporting accuracy, internal controls assessment, and management accountability. Independent audits and transparent financial disclosure requirements.

6. Local & Regional Regulations

Jurisdiction-specific compliance in all operating markets. Regular monitoring of regulatory changes and proactive implementation of new requirements.

7. International Standards & Best Practices

FATF recommendations, Basel III compliance, and emerging blockchain regulatory standards. Industry-leading security protocols and transparency measures.

Data Protection & User Privacy

User Rights

  • Right to access personal data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to deletion (Right to be Forgotten)
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to lodge complaints with authorities

Data Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for all sensitive data
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) enforcement
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Secure data backup and disaster recovery
  • Access control and role-based permissions
  • Regular employee security training
  • Data retention policies with automatic deletion

Transaction Monitoring & Enforcement

Real-Time Monitoring System

24/7 automated transaction monitoring with AI-powered anomaly detection. Suspicious patterns trigger immediate investigation and appropriate escalation.

Violation Procedures

  • Automatic flagging of suspicious transactions
  • Immediate notification to compliance team
  • Investigation within 24-48 hours
  • Regulatory reporting where required
  • Account restrictions or closure if necessary
  • Full documentation and audit trail
  • User notification within legal timeframes

Enforcement Actions

  • Transaction reversal for violations
  • Account suspension or permanent closure
  • Asset seizure in compliance with regulations
  • Regulatory reporting and cooperation
  • Legal action when appropriate
  • Zero-tolerance policy for repeated violations
